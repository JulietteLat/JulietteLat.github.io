Analyse de Valeur : le Forum de l'Ingénierie Durable

The “Forum de l’Igénierie Durable” (FID) is a student association at the UTC that organized in March 2019 the first internship fair and raising awareness forum inviting environmentally friendly organizations with social ethics only. After this first successful event, the FID team received many comments and the members started to disagree on several questions such as:

  • “How can we know if an organization is environmentally friendly?
  • Should we invite deeply committed organizations only?”
  • “What is our priority? Offering internships opportunities to UTC students or raising awareness on environment and social issues?”
  • “How could we improve our internal organization?”
  • “How could we expand our reach?”
  • Etc.

The project FID Value Analysis was led by a multidisciplinary group of five engineer students in order to answer to those questions and propose improved visions of the FID event for a time horizon up to 2020 and 2030. The group acted as a consulting team working together with the association for five months.
