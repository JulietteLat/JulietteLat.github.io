Analyse de Valeur : le Forum de l'Ingénierie Durable
Value analysis: the sustainable engineering forum

The “Forum de l’Igénierie Durable” (FID) is a student association at the UTC that organized in March 2019 the first internship fair and raising awareness forum inviting environmentally friendly organizations with social ethics only. After this first successful event, the FID team received many comments and the members started to disagree on several questions such as:
“How can we know if an organization is environmentally friendly?
Should we invite deeply committed organizations only?”
“What is our priority? Offering internships opportunities to UTC students or raising awareness on environment and social issues?”
“How could we improve our internal organization?”
“How could we expand our reach?”
Etc.
The project FID Value Analysis was led by a multidisciplinary group of five engineer students in order to answer to those questions and propose improved visions of the FID event for a time horizon up to 2020 and 2030. The group acted as a consulting team working together with the association for five months, using tools like octopus diagram, FAST diagram, causal analysis...

Urbanisme durable : le projet GOUTTE
Sustainable urbanism: GOUTTE project

The topic given during the "Urbanism and Environment" course was as follow: “Imagine an urban engineering project, device or service that could help face social and environmental issues in the future”. The group of three students I was part of, chose to propose a way to entirely transform any European mid-sized city to drastically diminish its energy consumption, its CO2 emissions in the global scale as well as its urban spreading, using concepts such as compact city, car-less city, bioclimatic architecture, urban agriculture… The purpose of this project is to show a way to organize the city to modify our lifestyles in a much more sustainable way, even dough it is not feasible considering current construction rules and means. It is more like an imagination exercise than a real concept.

Etude : rénovation thermique d'un quartier résidentiel de Compiègne
Study: thermal renovation of a residential neighborhood in Compiègne

For the "Urban Thermics" course, we studied the impacts of the thermal renovation of a neighborhood for Compiègne's town hall. The goal of the project was to modelize the current buildings in order to calculate their energy gains and losses, then to modelize the necessary renovations to meet the requirements of the "BBC rénovations" certification standards. Finally, an estimation of the costs of the renovation work and the returns on investments had to be provided.
We were four students working on the same neigborhood. First, we modelized the buildings with Autocad, then we imported this model in Heliodon and CitySIM in order to calculate their energy gains and losses due to gaography, solar rays and climate. In parellel, we modelized one typical house of the neighborhood in Pléiades so that we had precise thermal balance on this building, considering its materlials, its walls, floor and roof thickness, its ventilation and heating equipments... All the required information were provided by the town hall. The renovations were modelized by updating this unique model, and the costs and return on investment time were estimated based on the market prices and the local public statistics on the average income of households and accomodation fees.

Etude des effets de l'ombrage partiel sur une installation PV domestique
Study of the impacts of partial shading on a domestic PV plant

In order to better understand photovoltaic (PV) energy production and the issues encountered by serial PV cells, we lead a study on the effects of partial shading on small PV plants. The first part of this research job was to read a lot of scientific litterature on the topic. Since this subject had already been widely explored, it was hard for students like us to innovate. But we finally came out with an abacus to helping to calculate the daily power loss of a PV plant under daily partial shading conditions. This abacus may not be useful to anybody, it certainly demonstrate how easy it is for an unaware person to waste the potential of its domestic PV plant.

Essai sur l'approche de l'écologie en Chine

Lors d'un semestre d'études en Chine, j'ai essayé de comprendre au mieux ce pays sur de nombreux aspects. Si la complexité de son économie et de ses traditions restent encore largement hors de ma portée, j'ai tenté de synthétiser tous les éléments liés à son approche actuelle des enjeux l'écologiques dans un mémoire. Parce que l'écologie regroupe les enjeux les plus importants de notre ère, et parce que mon intérêt pour la Chine est accru par mes origines, je me suis particulièrement investie dans ce projet. Je remercie d'ailleurs Mr. Moulier Boutang, professeur spécialiste de l'histoire Chinoise, pour sa relecture attentive. Cet essai se base sur des documentaires et des ouvrages plus anciens, des rapports scientifiques, ainsi que sur ma propre expérience en Chine, mes études, mes observations, les informations glannées au détours de conférences et de discussions... Il en est à ce jour à sa troisième version, et est encore appelé à évoluer, à être complété.
